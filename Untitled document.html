The Science of 90 Minutes

Human sleep occurs in cycles. Each cycle lasts roughly 90 minutes. Waking up in the middle of a deep sleep cycle causes "Sleep Inertia"—that groggy, exhausted feeling. Somnus calculates the exact moment your cycle finishes so you wake up feeling refreshed and alert.
